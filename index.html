ClipVault

ClipVault is an AI clipping platform: multiple AIs detect the best moments and ship short videos to TikTok, YouTube Shorts, and Instagram Reels for creators worldwide.

What it does

  • Detects clip-worthy moments from streams and long videos
  • Auto-crops, captions, and formats for vertical short video
  • Publishes to TikTok, YouTube Shorts, and Instagram Reels

How it works

  1. Ingest stream/video
  2. Multiple AIs score and select highlights
  3. Generate captions and hashtags
  4. Ship to platforms with your metadata
